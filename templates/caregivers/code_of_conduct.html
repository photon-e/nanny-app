Digital Code of Conduct

As a caregiver on our platform, you agree to abide by the following code of conduct:

1. No Social Media

You are strictly prohibited from sharing any information about the families you work with on social media platforms. This includes photos, names, locations, or any details about the children or families.

2. No Spiritualism

You must not engage in or promote any spiritual, religious, or superstitious practices while caring for children. Maintain a professional, secular approach to childcare.

3. No Visitors

You are not permitted to have visitors, friends, or family members at the family's home during your caregiving hours. Only authorized individuals should be present.

Violation of any of these rules will result in immediate termination and may result in legal action.

By signing below, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to abide by this Code of Conduct.
